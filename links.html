Digital Legacy

Google Inactive Account Manager

https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/3036546?hl=en

Transfer Google data to someone else if you go inactive for a specified length of time.

Apple Legacy Contact

https://support.apple.com/en-gb/102631

Assign someone to be able to access your Apple data in the event of death, including iCloud files.

Microsoft Onedrive Legacy Contact

https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/preserve-your-digital-legacy-with-onedrive-245869c6-3505-4d75-a75f-82bdec48ad7b

Assign someone to be able to access your OneDrive cloud files in the event of death.

Facebook Legacy Contact

https://www.facebook.com/help/1568013990080948

Assign someone to make decisions about your Facebook account in the event of death.

Bitwarden Emergency Access

https://bitwarden.com/help/emergency-access/

An example of password manager functionality supporting digital legacy. Similar exists for Keeper, LastPass, NordPass and ProtonPass. All require paid accounts, not free.
