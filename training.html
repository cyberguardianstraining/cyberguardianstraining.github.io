Training Resources

These materials relate to training run by Northumbria University in a project funded by the Department of Science, Innovation and Technology (DSIT) for the Digital Inclusion Innovation Fund. They are free to use, but please let us know if you plan to use them by contacting James Nicholson at james.nicholson@northumbria.ac.uk. If you have questions about the materials or want to enquire about training, please also feel free to get in touch!
